## require.context(directory, useSubdirectories, regExp)
directory：说明需要检索的目录  useSubdirectories：是否检索子目录  regExp: 匹配文件的正则表达式
```js
const req = require.context('./components', true, /^\.\/[^_][\w-]+\/style\/index\.tsx?$/);
```
